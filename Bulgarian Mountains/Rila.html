Rila

The Rila mountain range is situated in the southwest of Bulgaria, about 60 miles (100km) from the capital Sofia. It is the highest range in the Balkans and one of the highest in Europe with over 130 peaks above 6500 feet (2000m). The highest is Musala peak reaching 9,596 feet (2,925m), making it the sixth tallest peak in Europe.
