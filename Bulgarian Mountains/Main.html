Bulgarian nature

Bulgaria is a country with a rich and varied nature. Its geographical location on the border between the temperate and subtropical zones physico largely determined the diversity of its natural conditions - climate, water regime, soil and vegetation cover. Another factor is the nature of the relief determined by geology and various morphotectonic structures.
